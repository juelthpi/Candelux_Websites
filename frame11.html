THE PROPERTY

Located in the enviable location of Cascais, only a stone’s throw from the beautiful beaches and renowned restaurants of Portugal’s famous Southern coastline, lies the The Stone Ocean Villa.

This spacious villa, designed by the famous Francisco Conceição da Silva, has room for 10 guests, featuring 5 spacious double bedrooms and a breathtakingly magnificent interior design scheme. Split over two floors, the villa is designed in wooden oak panelling that is juxtaposed with fresh white and cream sofa coverings and huge windows that shed the gorgeous Portuguese sunshine into the house at all hours.

The main living room acts as a central hub for you and all your guests and is a magnificent room.

Featuring floor to ceiling windows, huge plush sofas that can accommodate the entire party, and secret alcoves with book shelves nestled into their crevices for those of your party who may seek a quieter moment. Sloping glass windows complete with billowing white curtains face onto the glistening pool and gardens beyond.

There is also a fireplace in one of the sitting rooms. Behind these sloping windows is positioned the dining room table, which features enough seats for the whole party. Another dining table is located elsewhere in the house, with plush blue velvet chairs and feather sculptures on the walls to create a sense of mystery and excitement. The marble floors and wooden interiors create a feeling of subtle grandiose, and this is added to by the positioning of a wooden grand piano by one of the windows overlooking the greenery outside.

Days of total relaxation by the pool

A separate, smaller sitting room features an entirely white colour scheme and a TV, which is perfect for plunking kids in front of or for an evening of total relaxation. From here, steps lead to the upper floor of the villa. The apartment of the main house is located here, and it features a large master bedroom with its own bathroom and terrace. The other bedrooms are done up in a bright, refreshing, modern style, with white sheets and subtle colour schemes.

THE PROPERTY

Some feature private balconies with views of the ocean beyond. Bathrooms and bedrooms are done up with guests’ utmost comfort in mind.

A chapel, which has now been turned into a one bedroom en suite studio is the final piece of the puzzle for this villa, with its own private roof terrace complete with sunbeds and chairs.

The outside of the The Stone Ocean Villa is equally as beautiful. The swimming pool is huge, and its dark blue waters are truly alluring. With sunbeds located around the pool, complete with their own umbrellas, there’s space for everyone to kick back and relax. An outdoor bed is located at one corner of the pool, shrouded in white curtains and a roof overhead, making it the perfect place for a mid-afternoon nap for those who may have had too much sun! There is also a trampoline in the gardens to keep the children busy. There is also private parking located in the grounds of the villa.

As always, our five star concierge service is on hand to deal with your every request. A stay at the The Stone Ocean Villa feels like a stay in paradise.
