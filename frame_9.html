Escape. Explore. Experience...

Creating the most memorable experiences for our clients

CadenzaLux focuses on the rental of premium luxury, charming and unique homes, for short and medium stays in Portugal. With 15 years’ experience in the luxury market and with a wealth of knowledge working with properties in Portugal, Brazil and the UK, we take pride in the contacts and collaborators we have established worldwide.

A BROAD COLLECTION OF HOMES

Love for travel and our eclectic eye for detail

Since 2011, we have been involved in strategic marketing of resort developments in Brazil and developed and implemented sales and communications strategies for select properties in the run-up to and during the FIFA World Cup 2014 and the Rio de Janeiro 2016 Olympic Games.

From bohemian beach cabins to glorious palaces, the palette of properties we offer caters to all lovers of aesthetically designed homes and inspirational settings.

As well as short or medium-term stays, our select properties also cater for events or film and photo productions.

BESPOKE MANAGEMENT

Providing an individually tailored approach

Additionally, we look after homeowners and guests with the utmost care. With this in mind, we offer bespoke management to property clients and concierge services to our guests.

The Getaway Collection has recently embraced a new sector in realty, providing an individually tailored approach to sourcing and selling luxury and unique properties. Please visit our sales section for more information.
